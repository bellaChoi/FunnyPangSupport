FAQ

How do I clear blocks?

When a block and its neighbors add up to 10, they clear automatically.

I saw ads / rewarded videos. Why?

FunnyPang may show ads to keep the game free. Optional rewarded ads grant in‑game benefits. See Privacy for ad partners.

Does the game collect personal data?

We do not collect personal identifiers beyond what our analytics/ads partners provide (e.g., device or ad identifiers). See Privacy below.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑10‑28

FunnyPang may use third‑party SDKs for analytics and advertising. These partners may collect device data (e.g., ad identifiers, coarse location, usage events) to deliver services like crash reporting, performance measurement, and personalized or contextual ads.

Data We Process

  • Device & app info (model, OS, app version, performance metrics)
  • Advertising & analytics identifiers (e.g., IDFA/GAID) — if available
  • Gameplay events (level progress, session length, generic actions)

Your Choices

  • iOS: You can deny tracking permission (ATT). You can also reset the ad identifier.
  • Android: You can reset/limit ad personalization in system settings.
  • You can disable personalized ads where the ad SDK provides that setting.

Children

If the app is set to a child audience, we request child‑directed ad settings and show age‑appropriate ads. Parents can contact us for inquiries.
